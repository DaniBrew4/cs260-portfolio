  • Pose-Verification: Mutual
    For my Statistics and Data Science Capstone my team and I created a Machine learning algorithm using Nueral Nets to help increase scammer detection when new accounts are created. We classified and cleaned the data, trained the models, and set up a working api for Mutual to and recieve their responses.
